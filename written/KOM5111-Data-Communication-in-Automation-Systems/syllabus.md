## Objective

In today's technology, processes are controlled by distributed control systems
rather than by a single system. This makes data communication between control
systems essential and raises the importance of timely, correct communication.
The course addresses the data-communication structures between control systems
and examines the relevant technologies.

## Content

- Data communication
- SCADA systems
- Industrial communication protocols

## Learning outcomes

1. Identify, formulate, and solve data-communication problems with analytical reasoning; design and implement data-communication systems; and recognize industrial communication protocols.
2. Gain basic knowledge of Ethernet technologies.
3. Learn industrial communication protocols.
