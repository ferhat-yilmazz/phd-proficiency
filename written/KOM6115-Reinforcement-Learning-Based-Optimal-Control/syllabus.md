## Objective

Introduce reinforcement learning and build its theoretical foundation; relate
reinforcement learning to control theory; and apply it to the control of linear
and nonlinear systems.

## Content

Optimal control of Markov decision processes; dynamic-programming-based optimal
control of linear stochastic systems; reinforcement learning for Markov decision
processes; reinforcement learning for linear stochastic systems; function
approximation; (deep) reinforcement learning for nonlinear systems.
